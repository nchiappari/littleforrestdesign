About

Hi! I'm Eliza Forrest from Little Forrest Design. Since graduating with a degree in Horticulture from the Stockbridge School of Agriculture, I've been able to share my love of the natural world. My experiences include raising plants in a green house, landscaping on a large college campus, and working at a high-end plant and flower shop. Little Forrest Design allows me to express my creativity and share it with you!
