About

Little Forrest Design was founded in 2020 by Eliza Forrest, and provides event florals and landscape consultation and design.

Eliza graduated with a degree in Horticulture from The Stockbridge School of Agriculture, at UMass Amherst. Since then, she has gained years of experience working as a florist and landscape designer.

Eliza takes her love of color, texture, and movement and applies it to her work. She strives to capture the beauty of the natural world and make it accessible in a unique and unfussy way.
